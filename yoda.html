ABOUT:

Yoda was a male member of a mysterious species.
He was a revered Jedi Master who served as Grand Master
of the Jedi Order in the last days of the Galactic Republic.
He was famous within the Order for his wisdom,
Force powers, and lightsaber combat, and lived for almost 900 years.
